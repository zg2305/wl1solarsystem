Sun

Fun Facts

The Sun is a huge star in the center of our solar system. It’s mostly made of hydrogen and helium gas. The Sun’s surface is very hot, about 9,932 degrees Fahrenheit. It gives us light and heat, which are essential for life on Earth. Without the Sun, life on our planet wouldn't exist.

  • The Sun is a star in the middle of our solar system.
  • It’s made mostly of hydrogen and helium gas.
  • The surface of the Sun is super hot, around 9,932 degrees Fahrenheit.
  • The Sun gives us light and heat, which we need for life on Earth.
  • The Sun is really big, about 109 times wider than Earth.
