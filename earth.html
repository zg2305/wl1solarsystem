Earth

Fun Facts

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and has lots of life. It has different weather like rain, snow, and sunshine. The land has mountains, rivers, and forests, and there are big oceans too. The air around Earth protects us from the Sun's harmful rays. It's home to many plants and animals, making it a unique place.

  • Earth is the third planet from the Sun.
  • About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water.
  • Earth has one natural satellite, the Moon.
  • The planet's atmosphere is made up mostly of nitrogen and oxygen.
  • Earth is the only known planet to support life.
